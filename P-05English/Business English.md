#### Writing E-mails 

- **Starting:** 
	- Dear Mr/Ms [name]
	- To whom it may concern
- **First statement:**
	- <span style='color:cyan'>I hope that all is well with you </span> 
	- I'm writing to 
		- apply for the position of 
		- **inquire about**
		- ask further information about. 
- **Referring to previous contact**
	- With reference to our telephone conversation yesterday. 
- **Making a request:** 
	- Could you please send me. 
	- I am interested in.
- **Giving good news:**
	-  we are pleased to announce that
- **Giving bad news:** 
	- we regret to inform you that. 
	- unfortunately, we can't  
- **Complaining:**
	- I'm writing for complain about.
